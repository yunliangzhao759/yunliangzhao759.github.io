# 🏭 Nanjing Encos Motor Engineering  
### **Design & Structural Engineering Intern (2023–2024)**

---

# 🧭 Introduction

High-performance brushless motors and compact planetary gearboxes are foundational to industrial automation, robotics, and precision electromechanical systems. Improving motor efficiency, reducing backlash, and optimizing internal tolerances directly increases the reliability and accuracy of robots and automated machinery.

At **Nanjing Encos**, I worked on the design, analysis, and testing of custom motors and planetary reducers.  
**Objective:** Create compact, manufacturable, and high-precision actuator systems with smooth mechanical integration and reliable performance.

---

# 🛠️ Design / Methods

## ⚙️ Motor & Gearbox Mechanical Design
- Modeled rotors, stators, housings, and magnet assemblies in **SolidWorks**  
- Designed **5+ planetary reducer gearboxes** with varying torque/speed ratios  
- Created internal layouts for gear stages, bearings, and PCB interfaces  
- Conducted tolerance stack-up and clearance analyses for manufacturability  
- Optimized gear tooth geometry to reduce noise and backlash  

---

## 🏗️ Manufacturing, Documentation & Supplier Integration
- Produced detailed **GD&T engineering drawings** for CNC machining  
- Standardized drawing formats to improve supplier communication  
- Performed iterative teardown and rebuild cycles to validate manufacturability  
- Collaborated with suppliers to refine tolerances and improve assembly reliability  

---

## ⚡ Motor Tuning & Performance Validation
- Calibrated and tuned custom motors using **VESC firmware**  
- Performed controlled load tests to characterize torque, thermal behavior, and efficiency  
- Diagnosed and mitigated issues related to vibration, friction, and alignment  
- Used firmware parameters and mechanical adjustments together to improve performance  

---

# 📈 Evaluation / Results

## 🔍 Engineering Effectiveness
- Delivered multiple **working custom motor prototypes** for internal robotics platforms  
- Reduced **gearbox noise and backlash** through redesigned tooth profiles and spacing  
- Improved **torque transmission efficiency** with refined support structures and tolerances  
- Increased manufacturing yield by improving tolerance stack-up analysis  
- Enhanced documentation standards used by future engineering interns and full-time staff  

---

## 🔧 Impact on Workflow & Manufacturing
- Supplier feedback cycles became faster due to consistent drawing formatting  
- Assembly consistency improved through revised tolerance and fit guidelines  
- Early detection of misalignment issues prevented costly redesign cycles  

---

# 🧾 Conclusion

This project strengthened my experience in **precision mechanical design**, **CAD modeling**, **tolerance engineering**, **motor calibration**, and **performance validation**.  
By integrating mechanical design, manufacturing constraints, and firmware-level tuning, I contributed to custom actuator systems that were quieter, more efficient, and easier to assemble.

These improvements directly support the reliability and precision that modern robotic systems depend on.
